Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about StackArena.

What is Stack Arena?

Stack Arena (STAR) is a competitive gaming platform where players challenge each other in skill-based matches and tournaments. Players can stake Stack Coins and compete to win rewards.

How do matches work?

  • Choose a match or challenge another player
  • Both players stake Stack Coins
  • Play the match in the selected game (CODM initially)
  • Winner receives the prize pool minus platform fee

What games are supported?

At launch: Call of Duty Mobile (CODM). Planned future games include FIFA, Mobile Legends, PUBG Mobile, and other major competitive titles.

How do I deposit and withdraw?

Users deposit funds through securely supported payment methods to receive Stack Coins (SC). After winning matches, you can request withdrawals directly to your connected bank account.

What happens if a player disconnects?

If a player disconnects during a match, the opponent may report the match immediately. Final screen recordings/evidence may be required, and platform moderators will review the outcome securely.

Is cheating allowed?

Absolutely not. Cheating, exploits, or third-party software will result in an immediate match loss, account suspension, or permanent ban from the Arena.
