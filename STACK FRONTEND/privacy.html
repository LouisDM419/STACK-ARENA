Privacy Policy

Effective Date:

Stack Arena values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account we may collect:

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Game IDs
  • Payment information
  • Device and IP information

2. How We Use Information

Your data is used to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process payments and withdrawals
  • Prevent fraud
  • Improve platform performance
  • Provide customer support

3. Data Security

Stack Arena uses security measures to protect user data including encryption, secure servers, and fraud detection systems.

4. Sharing Information

Stack Arena does not sell personal data. Information may only be shared with payment processors, security partners, and legal authorities when required.

5. Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies and analytics tools to improve the user experience and monitor platform performance.

6. User Rights

Users may request to access their data, correct incorrect data, or delete their account. Requests can be sent to: support@playstackarena.com

7. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Users will be notified when significant changes occur.
